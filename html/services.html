Services

Vegetation Detection and Height Estimation

Ecolopper identifies and measures the height of individual vegetation objects within your corridors. This eliminates manual measurements and ensures accurate data collection.


Learn More

Growth Pattern Prediction

Ecolopper harnesses the power of cutting-edge machine learning to predict the future growth of vegetation near your power lines. This advanced technology accurately forecasts vegetation height, enabling you to proactively address potential encroachment.


Learn More

Risk Assessment & Alarm Generation

Ecolopper's algorithms combine current vegetation heights and predicted growth with user-defined thresholds to assess risk levels. This enables the system to trigger timely alerts for vegetation encroaching .


Learn More

Satellite Imagery Acquisition

Ecolopper acquires high-resolution satellite images of your power line corridors, providing a detailed snapshot of your current vegetation landscape. This comprehensive data serves as the foundation for all subsequent analyses.


Learn More

Visualization & Reporting

Ecolopper transforms complex data into easily digestible insights.Comprehensive reports provide clear visualizations of vegetation data, predicted growth, and risk zones. This empowers stakeholders to make informed decisions based on real-time information.


Learn More

Pruning Optimization & Scheduling

AI-powered insights recommend the most effective pruning schedules and prioritize critical vegetation objects, ensuring efficient resource allocation and minimizing unnecessary work.

About Ecolopper

Ecolopper empowers efficient vegetation management around power lines using AI. It predicts future growth, visualizes vegetation data, triggers alerts for risk, optimizes pruning schedules, and analyzes spatial risk zones. Ecolopper ensures grid safety, optimizes resources, minimizes environmental impact, and empowers data-driven decisions.
