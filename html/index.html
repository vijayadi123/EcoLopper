Growth Prediction and Forecasting

Ecolopper harnesses the power of satellite imagery and machine learning to predict the future growth of vegetation near your power lines. This advanced technology accurately forecasts vegetation height, enabling you to proactively address potential encroachment before it disrupts your grid.



Visualization Height Reports

Ecolopper provides comprehensive visualization and reporting tools to help you understand and manage vegetation growth around your power lines. These tools provide valuable insights and enable you to make informed decisions about vegetation management.



Alarm Generation System for Risk Detection

Ecolopper's AI-powered alarm system prevents outages by triggering real-time alerts when vegetation nears critical height, empowering swift action and safeguarding your grid for uninterrupted power delivery.



Pruning Decision Support System

Revolutionize your pruning strategy with Ecolopper's AI-powered heatmaps. These dynamic visualizations reveal the optimal timing and mode for pruning specific vegetation objects, empowering you to Ensure grid safety



Spatial Analysis of Risk Zones

Ecolopper empowers you to comprehensively analyze and understand vegetation risks with its advanced spatial analysis capabilities. This powerful tool provides valuable insights into potential hazards, enabling proactive mitigation and informed decision-making.

About Ecolopper

Ecolopper empowers efficient vegetation management around power lines using AI. It predicts future growth, visualizes vegetation data, triggers alerts for risk, optimizes pruning schedules, and analyzes spatial risk zones. Ecolopper ensures grid safety, optimizes resources, minimizes environmental impact, and empowers data-driven decisions.
